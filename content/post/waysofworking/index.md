## Hi, I'm Edd!

If you work with me, there are a few things I would like you to know.

### Why I'm Sharing This

Over the years, I’ve learned that how I work, how my capacity fluctuates, and how I communicate might be a little different from what people sometimes expect. Having this written down is my way of being as transparent and fair as I can be. 

It helps colleagues understand what they can rely on from me, what I may find difficult, and what I will and won’t tolerate in how people are treated. Clear expectations and boundaries mean we can get on with doing great work together, with fewer misunderstandings and less stress for everyone.

### Working Pattern & Availability

* **My Schedule:** I typically work [e.g., Monday to Thursday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM], mostly Monday and Wednesday WFH. 
* **Planning:** If we’re planning work around my offline hours, I’m very happy to agree on expectations in advance so nothing falls through the cracks.

### Health & Wellbeing

Not all medical conditions, disabilities, or personal circumstances are visible, and my capacity can vary from day to day. If I say I cannot do something, I am not being difficult or uncooperative; I am simply pacing myself so that I can continue to be at work and contribute reliably over time. If you have questions about what is or isn’t realistic for me, I’m always open to discussing it directly to agree on a plan.

### Communication: Email vs. Teams

Due to the nature of my work, I receive a very large volume of emails. 
* **For quick or urgent matters:** Email is unlikely to be the most effective route. A F2F or Microsoft Teams  chat or a direct call is usually much better. 
* **For highly time‑sensitive issues:** DRop by my desk / teams call.

### Be Explicit

I operate best with absolute clarity and don't typically 'read between the lines.' Something that may seem obvious or like common sense to others might not be immediately apparent to me. It's important that you make what you need from me **absolutely explicit** (e.g., exact software requirements, specific contents of a report, hard deadlines) so I can ensure you get the exact results you're looking for.

### Recording Meetings

I often record non-sensitive meetings so I can focus entirely on our discussion rather than on detailed note‑taking, using [Copilot / AI tools / transcription software] to generate a summary afterward. 
* I delete the recording as soon as the summary is produced (or it is automatically deleted after our standard retention period). 
* If you ever have any privacy concerns about recording a particular meeting, please just let me know in advance so we can agree on how best to handle it.

### Unacceptable Behaviour & Boundaries

Unacceptable behaviours include criticising individuals rather than their work (e.g., insults, mocking, or questioning personal competence), shouting, swearing, using an aggressive tone, or repeatedly interrupting, talking over, or dismissing someone. 

These behaviours are bullying and are **not acceptable** in a professional environment. I will challenge them where I can, and if necessary, I will raise concerns through the appropriate channels. Conversely, please do appropriately challenge *me* if you ever believe my own behaviour has fallen short of these standards or expectations.
